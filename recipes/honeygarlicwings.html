Honey Garlic Wings

Amazing Honey Garlic Wings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ib chicken winglets
  • oil for frying

Honey Garlic Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon garlic,minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 5 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seed scallion

Preparations

  1. Mix flour,garlic powder,pepper and salt in a bowl
  2. Coat each chicken wing with generous layer of mixture
  3. Pour 1/4 inch (6 mm) of oil into a pan. Bring to a medium heat
  4. In small batches, fry the chicken wings for 4 minutes on each side
  5. Remove from oil and let the chicken wings rest
  6. over medium heat, melt the butter and add the garlic and ginger
  7. add soy sauce,honey and brown sugar to the pan and reduce for 5-7 minutes,stiring occasionally
  8. add the fried wings directly into the suace mix until wings are completly covered with sauce
  9. sprinkel sesame seeds
  10. serve with scallions on top (optional)
