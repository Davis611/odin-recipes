Ponyo's Ramen

Studio Ghibli If you have ever wanted to try ponyo's ramen then you have found the right place, Down below you will find the recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of instant ramen (any kind of ramen you want)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 piece ham
  • 2.5 cups of water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients hard or soft boiled egg cut in half
  2. Boil water in a pot on the stove
  3. Place ramen noodles in a bowl if ramen noodles comes with season pour into the bowl
  4. Pour boiling water into the bowl.cover bowl and let sit for 3-5 minutes
  5. Once cooked uncover the bowl and mix well
  6. Add half a egg,sliced green onions and both pieces of cut ham on top of the ramen
  7. Enjoy
